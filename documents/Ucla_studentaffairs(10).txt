UCLA Student Affairs:
Where Bruins* Shine.
*If you are an alum, a retiree, a faculty member, a student, a staff member, or a parenta student, you are a Bruin.





UCLA, a vibrant campus life awaits. Full of enriching experiences that complement a rigorous academic environment, UCLA Student Affairs provides opportunities that propel you forward. Whether it’s learning to lead, joining a club, stepping out of your comfort zone, or gaining a new perspective on the world, the possibilities for growth and connection are endless.

As a Bruin, UCLA Student Affairs provides compassionate care to support your physical, social, and emotional well-being ensuring you can thrive both academically and personally.

Whether you're finding your path, exploring new interests, or seeking support, our services are designed to empower you every step of the way. Your journey as a Bruin is uniquely yours, and we're here to help you shine.

Student Affairs is full of mentors and advisors here to help you navigate this path.

Explore How To Stay Connected & Support Your Student
UCLA Student Affairs is your ally in aiding your student's journey. Research demonstrates that family involvement plays a pivotal role in student success, and when families actively engage in and support their student's university experience, it can have a positive impact. We are committed to partnering with you to ensure your student's well-being and success.

You are a valued part of the Bruin family, and there are numerous ways for you to engage, stay informed, and assist your student as they navigate their UCLA experience.

Connect with the Right Student Affairs Team, at the Right Time
UCLA faculty and staff members play a crucial role in direct student interactions and engagement. They are frequently the initial point of contact, where they may notice signs of student distress and become the vital link to help students access the necessary support at the right moment.

For guidance on how to assist students in maintaining their mental well-being and meeting their academic requirements, reach out to UCLA Student Affairs. We are dedicated to steering you in the right direction, making certain that all Bruins can fully utilize the wealth of available resources. Your students' well-being is our priority.

Resources for Urgent Needs

Be Well Bruin is students' hub for health and well-being resources. Get personalized recommendations using our self-assessment tools and to explore and discover a variety of resources.

Visit Be Well Bruin

The Economic Crisis Response Team (ECRT) is a multidisciplinary group of campus partners dedicated to supporting UCLA students experiencing financial crises that impact their well being and academic success at UCLA.
